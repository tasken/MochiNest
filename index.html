MochiNest

A calmer BLE workspace for your Pixl.js files

MochiNest is a client-side Bluetooth workspace for Pixl.js that uploads full folder trees, manages remote storage, and keeps slow device operations understandable while they run. It works entirely on top of the current firmware protocol, with no firmware changes required.
